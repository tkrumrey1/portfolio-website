Custom "Hackintosh" PC

Built in the case of a recycled 2005 Macintosh

Status: in progress

The goal of this project is to build a modern ATX computer inside of a 2005 Mac Pro case. The first step was to fully dissasemble the computer and remove all non-strucutral components. Then the case was powder coated white and painted with the 1997 Apple logo. After that the computer was reassembled and now I am in the process of 3D printing custom mounting for ATX computer components and integrating them into the case.
